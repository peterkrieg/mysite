Checkmate Pharmacueticals

My Dad's biotech startup needed a basic landing page site which I offered to create. Later, in the Spring of 2015, he decided to upgrade the site to have much more information. I worked on this from May-June 2015. It is the most recent website I have created, and the only that I actually feel proud of for a portfolio.

I created the website using the sass compoments and JS of Bootstrap 3. The initial logo was created by a graphic artist, but I was responsible for editing this logo later and tweaking the fonts and colors. I was responsible for the entire color scheme and look of the site.
