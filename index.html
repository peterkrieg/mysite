Checkmate Pharmacueticals

My Dad's biotech startup needed a basic landing page site which I offered to create. Later, in the Spring of 2015, he decided to upgrade the site to have much more information. I worked on this from May-June 2015. It is the most recent website I have created, and the only that I actually feel proud of for a portfolio.

I created the website using the sass compoments and JS of Bootstrap 3. The initial logo was created by a graphic artist, but I was responsible for editing this logo later and tweaking the fonts and colors. I was responsible for the entire color scheme and look of the site.

Sly's Barber Shop

In the summer of 2014, I was looking for practice making websites. I realized the barbershop I went to didn't have a website and I thought they could benefit from one. I proposed free haircuts in exchange for the website--what a great deal!

This site is based heavily on a bootstrap template, and is messy code. But I came up with the design, logo, and all photography. I managed SEO and eventually had the page getting 50+ unique visitors per week. Sly actually noticed an increase in business which was great.

My College Senior Thesis

I was a physics major at Bates College and needed to write a thesis in the Fall of 2014. At the time I was in the midst of learning JavaScript, and wanted a thesis that could combine my interests of programming with the mandatory physics material. I decided to write my thesis on physics mechanical simulations using JavaScript.

Basically my thesis was just a bunch of bouncing balls! But the code behind those bouncing balls involved JavaScript concepts like prototypes, inheritance, and window animations.
